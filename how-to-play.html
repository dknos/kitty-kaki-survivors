Kitty Kaki Survivors

How to Play

Controls

Move with WASD or the arrow keys; a gamepad left stick works too. Tap Space to jump and Shift to dash once the unlock lands. Weapons fire on their own — manual aim with the mouse is optional and toggleable in Options.

  • Move: WASD / Arrows / Left Stick
  • Jump: Space / A button
  • Dash: Shift / B button (after unlock)
  • Manual Aim: Mouse cursor (toggle in Options) / Right Stick
  • Pause / Options: Esc / Start button
  • Codex: F1
  • Perf overlay: F3

The Loop

Each run is a single life — survive a forest horde with auto-attacking weapons, pick a level-up card every time you fill the bar, and try to outlast the boss waves at 4, 8, 12 and 15 minutes. When you die (or claim victory), you return to the village hub: spend coins at the Shop, sigils at the House, and queue your next character + stage. Every run feeds the meta — cosmetic unlocks come from achievements, functional unlocks come from the Shop tree.

Weapons & Evolutions

Pick up the same weapon at level-ups to grow it (Lv 1 → Lv 6). At max level, stacking the right paired passive unlocks the weapon's evolution — a unique, named upgrade with its own visual identity. Examples include Holy Croissants → Toxic Halo, Magic Missile → Volley, Chain Lightning → Storm, Sticky Web → Tangle. The Grimoire modal on the start screen tracks every recipe you've discovered.

Characters & Signatures

Six characters unlock as you progress. Each one carries a starter weapon and a signature mechanic that changes how their runs feel:

Kitty
Nine Lives

One free revive baked in. Forgiving starter — best for learning the loop.

Boom
Charged Coil

Standing still charges a stored damage burst. Reward stutter-step play.

Webspinner
Lingering Silk

Webs persist longer and slow harder — kite-zone builds shine.

Sniper

Bonus damage to elites and bosses; ranged identity from frame one.

Phoenix
Ember Burst

Take a hit, exhale fire. Aggressive trade-melter for risk-takers.

Clockwork
Tempo

Attack-speed metronome stacks with every kill. Snowball into a buzzsaw.

Stages & Mutators

Three stages rotate the biome and the horde mix: Forest is the introduction; Twilight Hollow is the harder mid-game; Cinder Caverns unlocks after your first victory. The Daily Challenge locks the seed and character so everyone competes on the same run. The Weekly Mutator rotates every Monday — modifiers like Double Spawns, Glass Cannon, or Reverse Polarity change the rules for seven days. Boss Rush is a victory-lap mode once you've earned it.

Tips

  • Kite the swarm into a curve — your weapons sweep, so motion is damage.
  • Read the elite tells — ranged enemies stop to fire, leaping enemies grow a magenta marker, shielded enemies need their plate broken first.
  • Spend sigils on the Shop tree's tier-1 branch before chasing tier-4 capstones — early stacking compounds harder.
  • Open chests with confidence — the slot machine's double-or-nothing is friendlier than it looks.
  • Stack the right paired passive for an evolution; don't spread thin across weapons.
  • When you die, head to the village first — the shop tree is where build identity is bought.
